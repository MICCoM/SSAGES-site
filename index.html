About

This website hosts the software suites SSAGES (Software Suite for Advanced General Ensemble Simulations) and COPSS (Continuum--Particle Simulation Suite) which augment standard molecuar dynamics and Monte-Carlo codes, permitting particle--field coupling (COPSS), calculation of reaction coordinates and reactive pathways, and free energies (SSAGES). Each presents not only a set of methods, but also aims to provide a unified, extensible framework adaptable to generic molecular simulations.



IME codes SSAGES and COPSS are open-source and distributed under the BSD 3-clause license.



Seamless Integration

SSAGES and COPSS are designed from the ground up to connect to arbitrary molecular simulation codes through minimal programming effort. Initial releases target specific interoperability with open-source codes GROMACS and LAMMPS, and will grow to incorporate other open-source, proprietary, and bespoke codes.



SSAGES

SSAGES implements several methods for reactive pathway identification and free energy measurement, including Forward Flux Sampling, Metadynamics, and Nudged Elastic Band methods.



COPPS

The continuum--particle coupling provided by COPSS enables the spanning of multiple timescales in simulation.
