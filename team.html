Meet the Team



SSAGES Core Developers

SSAGES has primarily been developed by the groups of Juan J. de Pablo (at Argonne National Laboratory and the University of Chicago; pictured) and Jonathan K. Whitmer (at the University of Notre Dame; pictured). Those who have contributed code, with a brief summary of their contributions include:


  • Hythem Sidky (Whitmer Group), main architecture, core functionality, methods
  • Ben Sikora (Whitmer Group), main architecture, core functionality, methods
  • Julian Helfferich (de Pablo Group), main architecture, core functionality, methods
  • Yamil Colon (de Pablo Group), methods, project coordination
  • Cody Bezik (de Pablo Group), methods
  • Josh Lequieu (de Pablo Group), methods
  • Emre Sevgen (de Pablo Group), methods
  • Hadi Ramezani (de Pablo Group), methods
  • Ashley Guo (de Pablo Group), methods
  • Josh Moller (de Pablo Group), methods
  • Michael Webb (de Pablo Group), methods
  • Federico Giberti (Galli Group), methods, engine integration
  • Michael Quevillon (Whitmer Group), collective variables
  • Vikramjit Rathee (Whitmer Group), testing and validation








COPSS Core Developers

COPSS has primarily been developed by the groups of Juan J. de Pablo (at Argonne National Laboratory and the University of Chicago), Olle Heinonen (at Argonne National Laboratory; pictured), Dima Karpeev (at Argonne National Laboratory; pictured), and Juan P. Hernandez-Ortiz (at Universidad Nacional de Colombia; pictured). Those who have contributed code, with a brief summary of their contributions include:


  • Jiyuan Li (de Pablo Group), main architecture and core functionality
  • Xikai Jiang (de Pablo Group, Heinonen Group), main architecture and core functionality
  • Xujun Zhao (de Pablo Group, Heinonen Group, Karpeev Group), main architecture and core functionality
