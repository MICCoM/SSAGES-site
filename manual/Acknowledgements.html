Acknowledgments¶

We are grateful to Argonne National Labs for initiating this project and their continued support. Julian Helfferich acknowledges financial support from the DFG research fellowship program, grant No. HE 7429/1.

Some important core functionality of SSAGES comes from SAPHRON. SAPHRON - Statistical Applied PHysics through Random On-the-fly Numerics https://github.com/hsidky/SAPHRON
